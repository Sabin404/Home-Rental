Frequently Asked Questions

1.What is the process for renting a property?

To rent a property, start by browsing our listings. Once you find a property you're interested in, you can contact us to schedule a viewing. After viewing, you can apply for the rental by filling out an application form.

2.What documents are required for the rental application?

You'll need to provide a government-issued ID, proof of income, and references from previous landlords. Additional documents may be required depending on the property.

3.How do I schedule a maintenance service?

You can schedule a maintenance service through our website. Log in to your account, navigate to the 'Services' section, and fill out the service request form.

4.Are pets allowed in the rental properties?

Pet policies vary by property. Please check the property listing or contact us for more information about specific properties' pet policies.
